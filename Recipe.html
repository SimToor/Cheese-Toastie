My Recipe: Cheese on Toast

Cheese on toast is very tasty and especially good!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of brown bread (gluten free)
  • Cheese (lactose free)
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Grain of Salt
  • Sprinkle of black grounded pepper

Preparation

  1. Grill one side of the bread to desired taste and keep to one side to cool.
  2. Cut 3 or 4 slices of cheese.
  3. Place cheese on top of the toast and grill until melted (careful not to over-melt).
  4. Remove the cheese toasty and season it with salt and pepper.
  5. Enjoy with or without a dollop of Tomato Ketchup!
  6. Simple !
